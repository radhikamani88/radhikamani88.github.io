Instapark

It is very hard to find parking at University of Michigan during rush hours and during events. Many students have vacant garage spots and are willing to rent them for additional money.

Unfortunately, no centralized platform exists to facilitate these activities in seamless manner. Instapark is a solution to ths problem.
